# WhiteBird Dream Series

## English

### Overview

**WhiteBird Dream Series** is a mobility and aerial development series created within the activities of **Gravity Space-Time Laboratory**.

This series is not limited to a single automobile.  
It is a broader development concept that includes:

- ground mobility
- drones
- future flying objects
- AI-linked mobility platforms
- exhibition vehicles
- field deployment platforms

WhiteBird Dream Series is positioned as a clean, future-oriented series, distinct from the **Nautilus Series**.

While **Nautilus** represents the independent researcher’s personal philosophy, vintage-style identity, and deep conceptual world, **WhiteBird Dream Series** represents a cleaner, more deployable, more expandable mobility concept for future development.

---

### Concept

WhiteBird Dream Series is designed as a platform for:

- AI mobility development
- drone development
- humanoid-linked mobility
- future disaster-response mobility
- exhibition vehicles
- future flying object concepts
- practical field research platforms

The name **WhiteBird** expresses a clean, light, visible, and future-oriented image.

The word **Dream** expresses the long-term vision of the series:
a development path that begins with mobility, expands into drones, and continues toward future flying systems.

---

### Position Within the Laboratory

WhiteBird Dream Series is one of the major development series of **Gravity Space-Time Laboratory**.

This series is planned to evolve across multiple forms, including:

- **WhiteBird 1**: base ground mobility platform
- **WhiteBird 2**: drone development unit
- **WhiteBird 3 and beyond**: future expanded platforms

In this sense, WhiteBird is not only the name of a single vehicle.  
It is a series name for a family of mobility and aerial development systems.

---

### Philosophy

WhiteBird Dream Series is based on the idea that a mobility platform should not remain a simple means of transportation.

It can become:

- a research machine
- an AI implementation platform
- a field support vehicle
- a drone-linked system
- a humanoid support mobility
- a future flying mobility concept

This series is intended to connect present-day practical development with long-term future mobility imagination.

It is a research and creative series that can begin with a small vehicle, evolve into drone systems, and expand further into future flying objects.

---

### Naming Structure

- **Series Name**: WhiteBird Dream Series
- **Vehicle Name**: WhiteBird 1
- **Future Drone Name**: WhiteBird 2
- **Future Expanded Units**: WhiteBird 3, WhiteBird 4, and beyond

---

### Current Direction

At present, **WhiteBird 1** is planned as a clean white-based mobility platform with a future-oriented image.

This platform is intended to serve not only as a vehicle, but also as:

- an exhibition vehicle
- an AI mobility platform
- a development base for linked robotic systems
- a bridge toward future aerial mobility concepts
